# TECHNICAL ANALYSIS PRO — SYSTEM SKILL MODULE

---

## 1. PURPOSE

Defines deterministic, non-interpretive rules for technical indicator usage.

No storytelling. No “feelings of market.”

Only structured conditions.

---

## 2. CORE RULE

Indicators are NOT predictive.

They are **state classifiers**.

---

## 3. TREND LOGIC CORE

- EMA_20 vs EMA_50 defines directional bias
- ADX defines trend strength

Rules:

- ADX < 25 → no trend
- ADX ≥ 25 → trend valid

---

## 4. RSI USAGE RULE

- RSI ≤ 30 → oversold zone
- RSI ≥ 70 → overbought zone
- RSI alone NEVER triggers trades without confirmation

---

## 5. BOLLINGER BAND RULES

- Lower band → potential long zone
- Upper band → potential short zone
- Must confirm with RSI or ADX

---

## 6. VOLUME CONFIRMATION RULE

Trade validity requires:

- volume > SMA(20 volume) × 1.2 (minimum confirmation threshold)

---

## 7. ATR ROLE

ATR is ONLY for:

- stop-loss spacing
- volatility classification

ATR is NOT directional.

---

## 8. SUPPORT/RESISTANCE RULE

- Support = 50-period low
- Resistance = 50-period high

Breakouts require:

- volume spike
- volatility HIGH state

---

## 9. INVALID ANALYSIS CONDITIONS

Do NOT generate signals if:

- indicators conflict strongly
- volatility = LOW
- data is incomplete

Return: HOLD condition upstream

---

## 10. FINAL PRINCIPLE

> Indicators describe structure, not prediction.
